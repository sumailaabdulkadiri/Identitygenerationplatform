The TLU Digital student identity card is a unique identification card given to each student. The ID contains vital information such as the student’s name, photo, and a unique number.

Additional Security

The IDs can also have barcodes or smart cards for additional identification. Some of the places you can use your student card include school entrances and even classroom entrances.

Access Cafe and Vending Machines

The cards could even be used in a cafeteria or a vending machine system. Students can load money onto their cards and use their IDs to make on-campus purchases.

Advance Verification System

The advanced technology provides crucial information regarding students’ whereabouts and actions. So, what can you do with a student ID? The possibilities are nearly limitless.

Why you should have an Identity Card as a Tallinn University Student

The importance of a student ID card goes beyond identifying who belongs where. If you use the right card and program, the ID cards can be the most important asset for every student. You can use cards to get access to books and media items from the school library, or you can use them to buy lunch at the cafeteria or gain access to your classroom.
Tying ID cards with the systems your school already has in place will improve overall operations for students and staff.

Who is eligible for this Student Identity Card

If you’re a full-time or par-time student at Tallinn University, aged 14 or over, you can apply for a student card. We offers both virtual and plastic cards, allowing students around the world to instantly prove their official student status and access over 150,000 student discounts and offers worldwide.
